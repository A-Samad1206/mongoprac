Middleware are also called pre post hooks
Types of Mongoose MiddleWare
1.)Document MiddleWare 
   Fn supported by DM
   -  Init
   -  Validate
   -  Save
   -  Remove
2.)Query MiddleWare
   Fn supported by QM
   -  count
   -  find
   -  findOne
   -  findOneAndRemove
   -  findOneAndUpdate
   -  insertMany
   -  update
